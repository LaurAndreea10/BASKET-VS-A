How to play

  • Desktop: use A/D or arrows for aim, hold Space for power.
  • The aim slider and aim buttons are independent from power.
  • Bank shots can bounce off the backboard for bonus points.
  • Mobile: drag the aim slider, hold SHOOT, release near the gold zone.
  • Wins give XP, cash and fans. Shop upgrades improve assist, wind control and clutch shots.
